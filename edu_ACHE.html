Education ACHE

ACHE (American College of Higher Education)

American College of Higher Education (ACHE) is a pioneer higher educational institution in Sri Lanka and has proved 25 years of academic excellence. Broward College is accredited by the Commission on Colleges of the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools to award Associate and Bachelor’s degrees. ACHE is an International Center of Broward College, Florida, USA. Courses offered by Broward College at ACHE have been approved by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges (SACSCOC) in the USA.
