Fresno City College

Fresno City College (FCC or "Fresno City") is a public community college in Fresno, California. It is located near the Tower District in downtown Fresno. Also, it is part of the State Center Community College District within the California Community Colleges system. Fresno City College operates on a semester schedule, offering associate degrees and certificates.

Fresno City College has built upon its rich history and gone on to pioneer many new developments in community college education. Thousands of local people have worked through the years to make Fresno City College a viable and strong educational institution.
