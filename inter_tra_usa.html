This page is focus on my top tourist destinations in the United States. During the past 5 years I traveled to Knasas, Texas, Colarado, San Antonio, New York, New Jersey, San Fransisco, Nevada and so on.

Interests USA

New York

This is the biggest metropolitan city in the United States. New York city is a significant attraction site for tourists who visit the place every day. Empire State Building is yet another landmark in New York and a must see for any one visiting the city. Other attraction sites in the city include Times Square, museums, night life and restaurants among others.

San Francisco

San Francisco in California is a place full of scenic beauty and, therefore, it is a tourist destination that no one would like to miss.

Las Vegas

Las Vegas forms another big center for entertainment attracting people from all over the world. It has incredible recreation and entertainment places. The city also has beautiful natural scenery comprising of Grand Canyon, Zion and Death Valley.
