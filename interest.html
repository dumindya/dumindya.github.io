Interests

This page is dedicated to my interests and hobbies: arts and crafts, food, pet, travel, cooking/baking, listening to music, watching movies, gardening.

Interests Arts and Crafts

Art has always been a massive part of my life and also creative activities have always drawn my attention. I remember my first painting and I remember how I wished I had more time to make it better. Even as a child I strived for the best from my work. I prefer Fine Art to anything else, as fine art to me has more potential in it to embrace the artists’ emotions and can contain more depth and concept than any other media.

Interests Foodie

I have a deep and serious passion for food. Like most people, I love to eat but more importantly, I love to cook, talk about, look at and study food. I've recently discovered this new hobby of mine and if no one minds, I would like to talk about my interest. Initially, I started by just taking pictures of the great meals I was eating and keep them in my collection.

All types of cuisine inspire me whether it isEuropean, Asian, African, Middle Eastern, or American (Latin and North America). Not only cook food I’m also passionate about experiencing and travelling to various places to try new foods.

Interests Pet

I love animals/pets. For as long as I can remember I've always had an innate passion and fondness for and with pets. Pets are also incredibly cute, which is kind of a lame point to make, but it is still is an important part as to why I feel such compassion towards pets. As a child, I always had a special spot in my heart for animals and pets. I had a dog and also some cats who were like my best friends.

I traveled a lot when I was back home (Sri Lanka) and I have covered different states in America as well.
