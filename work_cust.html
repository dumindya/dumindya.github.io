Customer Service

This page talks about my experiences in customer service I received while I was working at Dunkin Donuts and Subway.

Work/Experiences Dunkin Donuts

Dunkin', also known as Dunkin' Donuts, which is an American multinational coffee and doughnut company. It was founded by William Rosenberg in Quincy, Massachusetts, in 1950. With approximately 12,900 locations in 42 countries, Dunkin' is one of the largest coffee shop and donut shop chains in the world. Its products include donuts, bagels, coffee, and Munchkins donut holes.

Work/Experiences Subway

Subway is also known as Doctor's Associates Inc. It is an American fast food restaurant franchise that primarily sells submarine sandwiches (subs), salads and beverages. It is one of the fastest-growing franchises in the world and, as of October 2019, had 41,512 locations in more than 100 countries. More than half its locations are in the United States.
